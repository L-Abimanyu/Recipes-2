Spatchcock Chicken

  • Ingredients

    1. 2 (3 1/2) pound whole chickens, wingtips removed

    2. 2 teaspoons salt

    3. 1 teaspoon dried tarragon

    4. 1 teaspoon paprika

    5. Â¼ teaspoon black pepper

    6. 4 teaspoons olive oil


  • DIRECTIONS


    1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a large rimmed baking sheet with foil.

    2. Place chicken, breast side down, on a work surface. Starting at the tail end, cut along both sides of backbone with kitchen shears. Remove backbone. Grabbing hold of both sides of the chicken, open it like a book. Turn breast side up

    3. Combine salt, tarragon, paprika, and pepper in a small bowl. Stir in oil. Run your fingers under chicken skin and rub tarragon paste under skin.

    4. Slide lemon slices under skin, in a single layer.

    5. Roast until skin is crisp and an instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of breast reads 165 degrees F, about 35 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting each chicken into 8 pieces.
